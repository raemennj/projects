Instructions

Cookie-Lator is a simple reference tool.

  • Clear All: Remove selected cookies from the total to start over.
  • Add Cookies: Click on a cookie card to add it to the total. (default)
  • Remove Cookies: Click on a cookie card to remove it from the total.
  • Items with zero quantity will be unavailable.
  • Complete Sale: Finalize the sale to update inventory and profit.
  • Manage Inventory: Add or remove cookie packages in your current inventory.
  • - Save Inventory: You must select this button to save changes to your current stock.
  • Sales History: View a log of all completed sales.
  • - Clear History: Remove all entries and start over.
  • - Reset Profits: Reset the total profit to zero and start over.
