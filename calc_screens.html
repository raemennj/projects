A tape-measure companion: combine inches, decimals, and fractions.

  • Chain math: 42 1/4 + 2.375 + 9/16
  • Results: red line on tape, mixed fraction (1/16″), precise decimal
  • Memory: long-press M1–M4 to save; tap to recall; MC to clear

Basics

Keys: + − × ÷ do what you expect. ← deletes a character; C clears.

Fractions: tap 1/16…15/16; typing 1 then 1/2 shows 1 1/2 in history.

Feet (Ft): type a number then tap Ft to enter feet; hold Ft 1s to preview results in feet.

Copy: tap a result to copy.

Repeat (↻): repeats the last op+term (enabled when valid).

Undo (hold ←): removes last “number + its operator”.

Results — 3 ways
  • Tape: red line marks exact decimal position (auto-fit view)
  • Left: rounded to nearest 1/16″ in mixed fraction
  • Right: precise decimal to 0.0000

When feet appear anywhere in the expression, plain inches are annotated with a ″ mark in history for clarity.

Memory Slots
  • Tap M1–M4: recall the value
  • Long-press / Double-click: save/replace the slot
  • MC: clears all slots
